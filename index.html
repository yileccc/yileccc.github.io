About Me

I am a research fellow at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working closely with Prof. Gao Cong and Prof. Han Yu. I obtained my Ph.D degree from School of Computer Science and Engineering at NTU, supervised by Prof. Gao Cong. Prior to NTU, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Chenliang Li.

I am broadly interested in data mining and data management, and their applications on spatio-temporal data analytics, smart city, and recommender systems.
