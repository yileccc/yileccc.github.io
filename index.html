About Me

I am a research fellow at The Singtel Cognitive and Artificial Intelligence Lab for Enterprises (SCALE@NTU). Before that, I was a PhD student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), advised by Prof. Cong Gao. Prior to NTU, I was an undergraduate student at School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Wuhan University under the supervision of Prof. Chenliang Li.

My research interests are broadly in data mining and deep learning. Specifically, I focus on spatio-temporal data mining and recommender systems.
